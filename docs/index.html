How it works

Core 0 — LoRa mesh

Listens on 868 MHz LoRa for messages from other nodes and relays them onward to extend mesh range. This node does not originate transmissions — it is a witness and a relay, not a sender.

Core 1 — probe sniffer

Passively captures 802.11 probe request frames — the constant, involuntary broadcasts every smartphone emits when scanning for known WiFi networks. MACs are SHA-256 hashed before storage. Raw addresses are never retained.

Display — OLED

Rotates every 5 seconds between mesh statistics, probe statistics, and a project statement. Hold the PRG button to freeze the current screen. Hold 2 seconds to clear session stats.

Critical Engineering

01

The Critical Engineer considers any technology depended upon to be both a challenge and a threat. Dead Reckoning treats the cellular and WiFi infrastructure surrounding it as the subject of study — not the medium of communication.

05

Each work of engineering engineers its user, proportional to that user's dependency upon it. Every probe request logged is evidence of a device — and therefore a person — being engineered by infrastructure they did not consciously choose.

07

The Critical Engineer observes the space between the production and consumption of technology. The moment a phone broadcasts a probe request is that space. Dead Reckoning acts there.

10

The Critical Engineer considers the exploit to be the most desirable form of exposure. The network was always watching. This device watches back — and shows you the count.
